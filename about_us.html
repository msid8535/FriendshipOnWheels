About us

Meet the team behind FriendshipOnWheels - we work hard to bring warmth, companionship, and nutritious meals to those in need. Our dedicated volunteers and staff are passionate about ensuring that every delivery not only nourishes the body but also lifts the spirit. We believe in the power of human connection and strive to make a positive impact in the lives of our clients by providing regular visits, friendly conversations, and a sense of community. Together, we are committed to fostering a supportive environment where no one feels alone or forgotten.
