Who we are

FriendshipOnWheels is a nationwide non-profit initiative which aims to help requestors (frail, aged people and people with disability) remain in their homes by providing them with meals, friendly visits and safety checks on a regular basis. Volunteers donate their time to deliver meals, friendly interactions and safety checks, whereas Requestors/Carers are those who receive the meal.


Our goal is to connect the community through food and companionship, fostering a sense of belonging and support for those who may feel isolated or vulnerable. Through our dedicated team of volunteers and the generosity of donors, we strive to make a meaningful difference in the lives of individuals who may be facing challenges in maintaining their independence. Together, we can build a stronger, more compassionate community where everyone feels valued and supported.

Work with your schedule, not around it

We understand that everyone leads busy lives. That's why we offer flexible volunteer opportunities that allow you to contribute to your community on your own terms. Our scheduling system is designed to accommodate your availability, ensuring that you can make a difference without sacrificing your other commitments.

Promote connections

One of the unique aspects of FriendshipOnWheels is the opportunity it provides to bridge the generation gap and promote intergenerational connections. Many of our volunteers are young adults or students looking to give back to their community, while many of our requestors are older individuals who may benefit greatly from the energy and enthusiasm of younger generations. By bringing together people of different ages and backgrounds, we foster mutual understanding, empathy, and respect. Through shared experiences and meaningful interactions, we break down barriers and create a sense of unity that transcends age differences. Join us in building a community where people of all ages can learn from and support one another.
