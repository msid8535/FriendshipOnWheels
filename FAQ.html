Helpdesk and FAQ

Welcome to the FriendshipOnWheels FAQ page, your go-to resource for answers to common questions about our services. Whether you're curious about signing up, meal options, or delivery schedules, find all the information you need right here to support your journey towards nutritious and convenient meal solutions.

How do I sign up to receive FriendshipOnWheels services?

Signing up for FriendshipOnWheels is easy! Simply visit our website and choose to sign up as either a volunteer or a requestor. For requestors, the process involves submitting information such as your dietary preferences so you can lodge meal requests with ease. For volunteers, this usually involves submitting your location and availability to volunteer.

What types of meals are offered by FriendshipOnWheels?

FriendshipOnWheels offers a variety of nutritious and delicious meals tailored to meet the dietary needs of seniors and individuals with disabilities. Our menu includes options for different dietary restrictions such as diabetic-friendly, heart-healthy, vegetarian, and more. Each meal typically consists of a protein, vegetable, starch, and fruit or dessert.

How often are meals delivered by FriendshipOnWheels?

FriendshipOnWheels delivery schedules vary depending on the location and program. In general, most programs deliver meals Monday through Friday, with some offering weekend or holiday meal options as well. Delivery times also vary, but meals are typically delivered during the late morning or early afternoon. Be sure to check with your local FriendshipOnWheels program for specific delivery details.
