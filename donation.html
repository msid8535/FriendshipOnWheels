FriendshipOnWheels relies on the generosity of donors like you to continue our vital work in supporting frail, aged individuals and people with disabilities across the nation. Your generosity enables us to recruit and train volunteers, purchase essential supplies, and expand our reach to serve more individuals in need. Together, we can make a positive impact on the lives of vulnerable members of our society and foster a culture of kindness and support. Please consider donating today to help us continue our mission of connecting the community through food and companionship.
